Offerings / Weekly Specials

Small morning specials with chai, kahwa, coffee, and seasonal food.

कलाNidhi is not being positioned as a full cafe. The food side stays personal: early-morning drinks, simple refreshments, occasional homemade dishes, and workshop-friendly servings from Rajeev Verma’s kitchen.

Rajeev shares this hobbyist food practice through HealthyFeasting on YouTube. Availability can change by day, so call or WhatsApp before visiting for food.

Hours: mornings from 5:00 AM to around 10:00-11:00 AM; evenings from 4:30 PM to 10:00 PM.

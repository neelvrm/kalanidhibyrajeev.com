About

Rajeev Verma: artist, author, mentor.

Rajeev Verma works across writing, mandalas, spiritual art, portraits, workshops, healing conversations, and simple morning rituals. His public writing at rajeevverma.org includes Hindi laghu kathas and reflective pieces on honesty, self-respect, relationships, gratitude, and everyday conduct.

His mandalas and paintings carry the same discipline: patient detail, symbolic form, and a steady handmade quality. The work ranges from dot mandalas and spiritual compositions to portraits, oils, decorative works, and custom commissions.

कलाnidhi is his Dehradun studio-gallery: a place to view art, browse books, commission personal work, attend workshops, and begin the day with laughter therapy, kadha, and chai.

Artist

Mandalas, portraits, oil paintings, decorative work, spiritual art, and custom pieces made with a quiet, hand-led discipline.

Author

Short stories, poems, reflective writing, guidance, and the book DAD, with many published titles available online.

Healer

Laughter therapy, gentle reflection, and conversations that help people return to breath, humour, and perspective.

Mentor

Small workshops for mandalas, dot art, painting, children, hobbyists, and people returning to creative practice.

Morning table

HealthyFeasting, kadhas, and everyday care.

Rajeev Verma is also a hobbyist cook and the person behind HealthyFeasting on YouTube. That part of his life will come into कलाnidhi as simple morning offerings: kadhas, chai, seasonal home-style preparations, and workshop refreshments.

This is not being positioned as a restaurant or a large menu. It is a personal, small-batch extension of the studio: food and drink as warmth, not noise.

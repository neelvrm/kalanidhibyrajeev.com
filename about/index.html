About

Rajeev Verma turns ordinary things into places to pause.

His public writing at rajeevverma.org is full of Hindi laghu kathas and reflective pieces about honesty, self-respect, kindness, relationships, gratitude, and the small choices that shape a life.

His art carries that same temperament. The mandalas are patient and meditative; the portraits, oils, decorative works, and spiritual themes feel rooted in observation rather than display.

Kala Nidhi is his open studio in Dehradun: a place to view art, browse books, sit for a conversation, learn slowly, and begin the day with laughter therapy, kadha, and chai.

Artist

Mandalas, portraits, oil paintings, decorative work, spiritual art, and custom pieces made with a quiet, hand-led discipline.

Author

Short stories, poems, novels, reflective writing, guidance, and the new book DAD, with many published titles available online.

Healer

Laughter therapy, gentle reflection, and conversations that help people return to breath, humour, and perspective.

Mentor

Small workshops for mandalas, dot art, painting, children, hobbyists, and people returning to creative practice.

Morning table

HealthyFeasting, kadhas, and everyday care.

Rajeev Verma is also a hobbyist cook and the person behind HealthyFeasting on YouTube. That part of his life will come into Kala Nidhi as simple morning offerings: kadhas, chai, seasonal home-style preparations, and workshop refreshments.

This is not being positioned as a restaurant or a large menu. It is a personal, small-batch extension of the studio: food and drink as warmth, not noise.

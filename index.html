What lives here

Handmade work, written work, and a morning rhythm.

Rajeev Verma’s writing at rajeevverma.org moves through everyday Hindi stories about honesty, self-respect, relationships, gratitude, and quiet moral courage. The same patience appears in his mandalas and spiritual art.

Mornings at Kala Nidhi are planned for laughter therapy, kadha, chai, and gentle conversation. Evenings are for art viewing, books, workshops, and visits.
